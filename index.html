Open Learning Project

Welcome. This site isn't a business portfolio, a pitch, or a corporate workspace. It's a simple, honest space designed for friends, family, and colleagues who want to learn how the web operates under the hood.

Most of us pay far too much for restrictive online ecosystems because the internet can seem intimidating. This project is my way of pulling back the curtain—showing the real financial numbers, real workflows, and free ways you can safely build your own custom websites, automated workflows, or simple data charts from scratch.

Take a look around, use the guides, and start building your own ideas.
